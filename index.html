Eric Xia


About me: I am a PhD Student in the EECS department at MIT. I am advised by Professor Martin J. Wainwright, and also work closely with Professor Whitney Newey. I am fortunate enough to be supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship. Prior to MIT, I was at UC Berkeley, where I received my bachelors in Computer Science in 2020, and masters in Statistics in 2022.

Research interests: I work on statistical theory and methodology with an emphasis in addressing issues quantitative researchers in other disciplines tend to face.
